Cell Simulator: by Aditya Suresh

Instructions: (click on the screen below, these particles will appear wherever your mouse is on the canvas)

Press 1 to create a body cell (regular cell which can get infected by viruses)

Press 2 to create a macrophage (eats viruses attached with antibodies an bacteria)

Press 3 to create a killer T cell (kills infected cells)

Press 4 to create a b_cell (creates antibodies)

Press 5 to create a helper T cell (helps deliver parts to trigger immune response from dendritic cells)

Press 6 to create a dendritic cell (eats a pathogen and delivers the digested parts to a helper T cell)

Press q to create a cold virus (infects body cells and reproduces)

Press w to create staphylococcus aureus (a bacteria which creates toxins to toxinate and kill body cells and macrophages)
